Looking for data to use in your next project? Here are some good sources for spatial data:

### Detroit & Michigan
* [City of Detroit](https://data.detroitmi.gov/)
* [Data Driven Detroit](http://d3.d3.opendata.arcgis.com/)
* [Detroit Data](www.detroitdata.org)
* [Michigan Geographic Data Library](http://www.mcgi.state.mi.us/mgdl/?rel=ext&action=sext)
* [Michigan Department of Natural Resources](http://alpha.midnr.opendata.arcgis.com/)
* [Motor City Mapping](https://www.motorcitymapping.org/)


### United States & Canada
* [University of Windsor guide to Canadian data](http://leddy.uwindsor.ca/adc/geospatial-data-canada-dataportal)
* [US Census TIGER/Line 2014](https://www.census.gov/cgi-bin/geo/shapefiles2014/main)

### Global
* [Natural Earth](http://www.naturalearthdata.com/)

### US Demographic
* [American FactFinder, Advanced Search](http://factfinder.census.gov/faces/nav/jsf/pages/searchresults.xhtml?refresh=t)
* [LEHD Employment Statistics](http://lehd.ces.census.gov/data/)
